Cookie Dough Basics

When dough is made at home, common ingredients include flour, eggs, butter, white sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract,and salt. Along with a basic dough recipe, there are many things you can add to the dough to change the flavor profile. Things such as spices or extracts will change the taste of the dough as one. However, mix ins such as chocolate chips, m&m's, candy bars or even fruits add more of a texture to the cookie.

Snickerdoodle Cookies

A snickerdoodle is a type of cookie made with flour, fat, sugar, and salt, and rolled in cinnamon sugar. Eggs may also sometimes be used as an ingredient, with cream of tartar and baking soda added to leaven the dough.

    Step 1: Make the topping by mixing together white sugar and cinnamon.
    Step 2: Make cookie base: whisk the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, cinnamon and salt together.
    Step 3: Add the eggs, softened butter and vanilla extract to the mixture.
    Step 4: Form balls of dough.
    Step 5: Coat the cookie dough with the cinnamon and sugar mixture.
    Step 6: Bake at 350 degrees for 12-14 minutes.
